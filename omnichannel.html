Omnichannel Communication

Engage debtors on their preferred channels with our integrated SMS, WhatsApp, and Email platform.

Overview

In today's digital world, effective communication requires a multi-channel approach. Our Omnichannel Communication platform allows you to reach debtors through the channels they use most, increasing engagement and recovery rates.

By automating outreach through SMS, WhatsApp, and Email, we ensure consistent messaging and timely follow-ups, all managed from a single unified interface.

Key Features

Bulk SMS Scheduling

Automated SMS campaigns with personalized templates and smart delivery optimization for high open rates.

WhatsApp Integration

Verified WhatsApp Meta templates for professional and interactive debtor engagement with rich media support.

Automated Emailing

Scalable email marketing with tracking, bounce management, and responsive templates tailored for debt recovery.

Unified Inbox

Manage all debtor responses from different channels in one place, ensuring no communication is missed.

Business Benefits

  • Improved debtor response rates via preferred channels
  • Reduced communication costs compared to manual calls
  • Automated follow-ups for consistent recovery efforts
  • Enhanced brand professionalism with verified templates
  • Detailed tracking and reporting of communication success
  • Scalable outreach for large debt portfolios
