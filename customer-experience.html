Customer Experience

Recovery with empathy. We prioritize ethical practices and positive customer interactions to protect your brand and maintain customer dignity.

Overview

At Drexorra, we believe that how a debt is recovered is just as important as how much is recovered. Our Customer Experience module is centered on empathy, ethics, and professional communication.

We work to find mutually beneficial solutions for both our clients and their customers, aiming to resolve debts while preserving the customer relationship and your brand's reputation in the marketplace.

Key Features

Empathy-First Approach

Training specialized in identifying vulnerable customers and utilizing compassionate negotiation techniques to find sustainable payment solutions.

Ethical Collections

Strict adherence to a code of conduct that prohibits harassment and ensures transparency in every step of the recovery process.

Escalation Matrix

Clear internal procedures for handling complex cases and customer grievances quickly and fairly.

Win-Win Strategies

Creative settlement options and structured repayment plans designed to return the customer to good standing.

Business Benefits

  • Preservation of customer relationships and brand loyalty
  • Significant reduction in customer complaints and legal risks
  • Improved long-term recovery through sustainable settlements
  • Positive brand positioning in a high-scrutiny industry
  • Enhanced customer cooperation through respectful treatment
  • Continuous UX improvement based on debtor feedback
